# Learner Entry

If you are new to this project, do not start by reading every file.
Start by understanding the learning path.

## Step 1
Read:
- `README.md` or `README.zh-CN.md`
- `docs/project-positioning.md`

## Step 2
Understand why these two topics matter:
- `docs/environment-importance.md`
- why DQN and PPO are the chosen core algorithms

## Step 3
Follow the learning order:
- `docs/learning-path.md`
- DQN step files
- PPO step files

## Step 4
Use these when something feels off:
- `docs/debug-boundaries.md`
- `docs/expected-behavior.md`

## Step 5
After running or reading an example:
- `docs/experiment-review.md`

## Rule
The goal is not to finish reading the repository.
The goal is to leave with a more stable understanding of why RL training behaves the way it does.
