# Track Overview

Hands-on RL currently has two core tracks:

## Track 1: DQN
Use this track to understand value-based reinforcement learning.

Entry points:
- `DQN/README.md`
- `DQN/learning-map.md`
- `examples/cartpole_dqn.py`
- `examples/cartpole_dqn.md`

## Track 2: PPO
Use this track to understand policy optimization and actor-critic learning.

Entry points:
- `PPO/README.md`
- `PPO/learning-map.md`
- `examples/lunarlander_ppo.py`
- `examples/lunarlander_ppo.md`

## Suggested order
If you are new, go DQN first, then PPO.

## Why
The two tracks are not duplicates.
They represent the two most important RL learning routes.
